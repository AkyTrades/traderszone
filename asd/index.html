FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly is TradersZone?

A marketplace for trading education. Independent traders list their courses and Discord communities here; you preview their content for free and join the ones you like through us.

How do you vet traders?

Before listing, we review each trader's content quality, the transparency of their claims, and feedback from their existing members. We remove traders who don't hold up.

Why buy through TradersZone instead of directly?

Same price, more protection. You get one trusted checkout, and if a course or community isn't what was advertised, you have us to escalate to instead of an anonymous DM.

Are the free clips really representative?

Yes — clips are pulled from the actual courses and communities, not made-for-marketing trailers. What you see is how they teach.

Do any of these traders guarantee profits?

No, and anyone who does gets removed. Trading involves substantial risk of loss. Everything here is education, not financial advice.
